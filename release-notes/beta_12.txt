# beta_12 — GPS hotfix + UI follow-up (one user-facing note per non-blank, non-# line; # lines are section comments)

# --- GPS fix (headline) ---
Fixed GPS being slow to get a fix — or sometimes never acquiring at all — on the T-Deck. The GPS receives its data over a small serial buffer, and on the busy touch UI a heavy screen redraw could stall long enough to overflow it and drop the satellite data mid-stream, which forced the receiver to start over. The buffer is now 16x larger, so those redraws no longer cost you the fix — acquisition is back to normal (a fix in about a minute instead of several, or none).

# --- Sensors / awareness ---
New Sensors tab sits between Map and Settings and brings the local environment, GPS, battery, terrain alerts, altitude trend, track recording guard, and GPS fix/loss status together in one place.
The Home and control surfaces now expose rotation state more clearly, with a compact rotation chip plus a persistent rotation-lock toggle in Display settings.

# --- Map / waypoints ---
Map tile downloads now have a proper queue manager: pause and resume downloads, retry failed tiles, queue the current map area, and clear finished jobs without leaving the device UI.
Incoming `WDMWP|...` messages can now import waypoints directly into the local map, with duplicate coordinates updated instead of creating clutter.

# --- Stability ---
Several follow-up stability fixes landed across the touch UI: safer buffers, fewer race conditions, and more reliable waypoint persistence across reboot.
